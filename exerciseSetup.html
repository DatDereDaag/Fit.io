Please select your desired workout split:

Push Pull Legs(PPL): A more advanced workout split for those who can spare the extra time and have enough energy to train for 6 days at most for the week.Offers the most returns on muscle and peak aesthetics as push muscles, pull muscles and leg muscles are equally and effectively worked.

Upper-Lower: An intermediate workout split for those who have a moderate amount of time and energy to train for 4 days at most for the week. Offers an optimal return on muscle gained with not much energy being needed to be dedicated to training. The upper body is targetted on one day while the legs will be targetted on another with enough rest being given in between.

Full Body: A beginner friendly workout for those who are new to the gym and have yet to make their first few gains in muscle mass. Little energy and time is needed as only 3 days of the week are spent training. With the entire body being worked each day and adequate rest being give in between routines. Returns on muscle gains may plateau after the firt few months as the extra training needed for each muscle group will not be provided.
